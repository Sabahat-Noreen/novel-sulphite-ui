About the Narrative

Written by Noor Rajpoot, Sulphite delves into the complexities of human emotion, trauma, and the haunting beauty of brokenness. The prose is melodic yet sharp, mirroring the volatility of the chemical it is named after.

The story resonates with those who find solace in the shadows and seek the truth behind the masks we wear. It is a journey through the corridors of a mind grappling with its own existence.
